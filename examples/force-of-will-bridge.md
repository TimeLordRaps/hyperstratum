# Force of Will Bridge

Force of Will is downstream of Hyperstratum.

Hyperstratum defines the broader recursive-form ontology. Force of Will defines a structured agency model:

```txt
W_a(t) = <P_a(t), N_a(t), F_a(t), I_a(t), V_a(t), R_a(t)>
```

Where:

```txt
P = past will
N = present / now-will
F = future will
I = invariant will
V = variable will
R = relational will
```

## Import boundary

Force of Will may use `hypernode`, but it should not need to define the entire hyperstack.

Bridge definition:

```txt
In the Force of Will repo, a hypernode means a local recursive agency-node that can be modeled through the six-will structure. The full ontology belongs to Hyperstratum.
```

## Person-hypernode and will

A person-hypernode can be modeled as will-bearing when its agency is represented through the six-will structure.

```txt
PersonHypernode -> W = <P, N, F, I, V, R>
```

Where the person's self-mira-recursive-graph may inform their invariant will, relational will, variable will, and future will without being reducible to social reception.

## Identity warning

Force of Will should preserve this Hyperstratum distinction:

```txt
self-mira-recursive-graph != social-mira-graph
```

Therefore:

```txt
Social reception may affect variable will and relational will.
Social reception is not invariant will.
Social reception is not core identity.
```
